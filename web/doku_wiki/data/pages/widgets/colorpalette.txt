====== Color Palette ======

The Color Palette widget's purpose to to save a bunch of colors (a.k.a swatches) for the user.  It can be though of as a [[widgets:colorchoosers|Color Chooser]] widget.  Colors are saved in the same directory as the rest of the files.

  * It is stateless
  * It is a subclass of QWidget
  * It contains a bunch of [[widgets:colorframe#clickable_color_frame|Clickable Color Frame]] instances to act as the swatches
