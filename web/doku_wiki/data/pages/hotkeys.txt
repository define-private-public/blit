====== Hot Keys ======
Below are a collection of default hotkeys in Blit.

  * Qt::Key_J -- Goto beginning of the animation
  * Qt::Key_K -- Play/Pause animation
  * Qt::Key_L -- Goto end of the animation
  * Qt::Key_Comma -- Move one Frame left
  * Qt::key_Period -- Move one Frame right
  * Qt::Key_Minus -- Hold -= 1
  * Qt::Key_Equals -- Hold += 1
